About Me

My name is Josiah Dover, I'm from Chattanooga, TN. I received a bachelor's in Chemical Engineering from Tennessee Tech in 2016. I had multiple years of laboratory research experience, which led me to work as an Organic Research Chemist after graduating.
After three years working in the pharmaceutical industry, I traveled with my partner as she worked travel nurse contracts over the pandemic. I gradually immersed myself in web languages over that time, which culminated in completing an online coding bootcamp and receiving the certifications listed in my skills.
I'm an enthusiastic learner who is always intrigued by details, intricacies, and concepts. These characteristics have allowed me to excel as a chemist, and I believe they are what propel me towards being a critically thinking web developer. I also enjoy writing and playing music and delving into the creative processes involved with doing so. This quality is also very important in design, and my enjoyment of creativity allows me to focus on UX/UI in unique way. Thanks for visiting my page, and I look forward to brainstorming on our next project!
